A husky is a sled dog used in polar regions. One can differentiate huskies from other dog types by their fast pulling-style. They represent an ever-changing cross-breed of the fastest dog. Humans use huskies in sled-dog racing. In recent years companies have marketed tourist treks with dog sledges for adventure travelers in snow regions. Huskies are also kept as pets, and groups work to find new pet homes for retired racing and adventure-trekking dogs
