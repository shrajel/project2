The Chow-Chow has been identified as a basal breed that predates the emergence of the modern breeds in the 19th Century.One writer has proposed that the Chow-Chow originated in China 2,000 years ago or originated in Arctic Asia 3,000 years ago and then migrated to Mongolia, Siberia and then to China.
